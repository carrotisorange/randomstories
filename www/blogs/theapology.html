I’m lucky I still this lastlast paper from the first letter I gave to you and thought this might cheer you up. This is just a freestyle writing. I’ll write everything that pops out on my head. Please bear with my calligraphy. It’s been so long since I wrote you one. Lol You’re a strong woman, loving daughter, sweet sister to your siblings and to your pamangkins as well, a good friend, and a thoughtful girlfriend.
